Honey Garlic Chicken

Description

Are you looking to impress all your friends with your grilling skills? Nothing will blow their taste buds out of water like these Grilled Honey Garlic chicken thighs.

Ingredients

  • 4 large boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/3 cup of honey
  • 1/3 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar
  • 4 garlic cloves, smashed or minced
  • Fresh thyme
  • 1 tablesppon smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Add honey, soy sauce, garlic, apple cider vinegar, paprika and thyme to a bowl and mix well until it is completely incorporated
  2. Grab a gallon sized resealable bag. Added chicken thighs and marinade mixture. Be sure to set aside about 1/4 cup for basting later.
  3. Massage mixture into chicken.
  4. Place in fridge and let marinate for at least for 2-3 hours if not over night.
  5. Heat one side of grill up to medium heat, leaving a cooler side for indirect cooking.
  6. Place marinated chicken onto the direct heat side of grill and let cook for 5 minutes each side, ensuring the marinade does not burn.
  7. Move chicken to cool side and put cover back on grill
  8. Bast the extra marinade onto chicken every 3-4 minutes until chicken is cooked, about 10 more minutes.
  9. Once cooked, pull from grill, and let rest for about 5 minutes
  10. Serve with preferred sides, and enjoy!
