Lemon Pepper Chicken

Home

Description

Lemon Pepper chicken is a refreshing zesty meal served over rice, that everyone will enjoy, and will enjoy how healthy it is. By cooking it all in one pan, it makes for a quick, easy meal that takes minimal cleanup.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 lemon cut into slices
  • 1 chopped yellow onion
  • Lemon pepper seasoning
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • Either 1/2 pound of asparagus or broccoli, whichever you prefer.
  • 2 1/4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of long grained rice

Steps

  1. Begin by cleaning of chicken breast and then adding lemon pepper seasoning to chicken. Put a generous amount of chicken.
  2. Add chicken to a hot pan and cook on both sides until brown, about 3-4 minutes. Chicken does not need to be fully cooked for this step, as it will finish cooking in another step, and we do not want to chicken breast to dry out.
  3. Once chicken breast is browned, pull it out,set it to the side, and add garlic and onion to pan.
  4. Once garlic and onion is added and browned, add rice and toast for 30 seconds.
  5. Add chicken broth to rice and bring to a simmer. Add a teaspoon of lemon pepper seasoning. Add chicken back in and let cook for 10 minutes covered on low heat.
  6. Stir in asparagus or broccoli, place lemon slices ontop of chicken, cover pan and let cook for 5 additional minutes.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
