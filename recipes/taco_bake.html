Taco Bake Recipe

Home

Description

Taco Bake is a simple dish that brings the contents of a taco into a cassorole like dish that the whole family will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 16 ounce can of black beans (low sodium if possible).
  • 1 16 ounce can of pinto beans(low sodium if possible).
  • 1 3 ounce can of green hatch chilis.
  • 1 pack of taco seasoning, your choice of heat of seasoning.
  • Half of a chopped yellow onion
  • 16 ounce tub of sour cream.
  • 16 ounce bag of cheese, chedder or a fiesta blend.
  • 1 bag of tortilla chips

Steps

  1. First add onion into a pan over medium heat. Begin to get some color on onions before adding ground beef.
  2. Once onions have a nice color, add ground beef, and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook until brown and then drain grease from the pan.
  4. Drain water from the black beans and pinto beans.
  5. Add green hatch chilis, the beans, and taco seasoning to the pan with the ground beef and mix them together.
  6. Once mixed together, cook for 3-5 minutes until the beans are warm.
  7. Flatten out the mixture, then add the sour cream on top, and spread it out evenly.
  8. Add cheese on top of the sour cream, then put lid on pan and cook on low heat until cheese is melted.
  9. Take pan off heat, serve and enjoy with tortilla chips on the side!
